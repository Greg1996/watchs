Campus Stories are a special 'Our Story' — created just for select college campuses! Only Snapchatters whose phones show that they’re around campus, or were there in the last 24 hours, are able to post to that Campus Story. You’ll have to turn on location services so that Snapchat can see if you’re eligible to take part in a Campus Story. Keep in mind that sometimes the accuracy of any device’s location information can be affected by the operating system and other apps you have.

OUR STORY

Begun by the author when he was eighty-seven years old and mourning the loss of his wife, Our Story is a graphic memoir like no other: a celebration of a marriage that spanned the twentieth century in China, told in vibrant, original paintings and prose. Rao Pingru was twenty-four-year-old soldier when he was reintroduced to Mao Meitang, a girl he’d known in childhood and now the woman his father had arranged for him to marry. One glimpse of her through a window as she put on lipstick was enough to capture Pingru’s heart: a moment that sparked a union that would last almost sixty years.
